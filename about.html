WE ARE FAMILY

2 sisters (with a lot of help from brothers, parents, spouses, children, nieces, nephews, neighbors, and lots and lots of friends) Robin and Amy started their own short term rental company in 2016. One of the first AirBnb's in the area, Robin and Amy's A Li'l Love Inn, LLC sought to help cover the upkeep of the cottage their parents had handed down to them. Our success was a surprise, but also a result of a LOT of hard work (and a LOT of FUN), creative juices, and God's blessings of nature and beauty. Since the first day our doors opened (and multiplied), we have aimed to provide our guests with comforts and recreation you won't find at most rentals, with everything you need on hand, with tasteful and creative decor, and lots of communal areas perfect for memory making with loved ones. 6 siblings, 14 grandchildren, 28 great grandchildren and cousins galore have helped us test our offering. We hope you'll join us!!!

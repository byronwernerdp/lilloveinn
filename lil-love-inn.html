Whimsical & Wonderful.

The namesake cottage of the property — charming, cozy, and full of personality. Perfect for a couple's getaway or a small group of friends looking to unwind in nature.

The back deck overlooks the lake and is the perfect spot for morning coffee or evening cocktails. Fire up the firetable, swing in the hammocks, or challenge friends to a round of ping pong.

Great WiFi for remote work stays. Unique whimsical decor throughout.

The Space

This cozy cottage has everything you need for a memorable stay. The sleeping porch provides a magical way to drift off to the sounds of nature, while the basement offers additional sleeping space for extra guests.

Comfortable living area, well-stocked kitchen, and all the outdoor amenities you could want — firetable, firepit, hammocks, and ping pong table.

Want friends or family to join? Check out our neighboring properties — we can accommodate up to 30 guests total for family reunions, retreats, and special events.

Please note: We no longer have access to kayak or to use the lake for recreational use. We have excellent options for swimming and kayaking nearby.

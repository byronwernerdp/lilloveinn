For travelers looking for a peaceful, comfortable setting, join us in Northwest Pennsylvania in the historically rich, rural town of Cambridge Springs, Pennsylvania. The scenery is stunning, constantly changing, and entertaining as a source of beauty throughout your stay. We aim to provide our guests with comforts and quality that make us unique in this area. With everything you need on hand, tasteful and creative decor, and communal space designed to bring visitors together, your stay is one everyone will remember.

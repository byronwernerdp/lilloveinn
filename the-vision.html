Inquire About The Vision

Everyone Can Come Together.

When you need to bring the whole crew, The Vision makes it possible. All three neighboring properties rented together, with so many communal areas and bedding options to suit all of your guests.

10+ bikes, 4 fire pits, 2 propane fire tables, 4 large expansive decks, 2 finished basements, comfy beds everywhere. Excellent WiFi, Sonos speakers, new appliances, stocked toy room, ping pong, corn hole, air fryers.

Golf courses, ski resort, craft brewery within minutes. All you need to bring are clothes and food. Wildlife, nature, serenity, and lifelong memories await!

The Space

We rent two or three of the homes at a time, subject to availability. All have gorgeous views of the lake with private outdoor space, perfect for intimate or large gatherings. The decor is comfortable and artistic, lending to a very memorable and relaxing time.

The home is on a man-made lake located near Cambridge Springs and Edinboro. 4 great golf courses are within 5 miles, including Riverside (1 mile), Venango Valley, Country Meadows, Culbertson's, and more!

Activities include antiquing, kayaking on nearby French Creek, Riverside Brewing Company for music and craft beer, playgrounds, and hikes. Within 30 minutes: a casino, spa, indoor & outdoor water parks, Waldameer amusement park, Millcreek Mall.

During winter, you can see Mt. Pleasant ski resort from the back deck — just 6 minutes away.

Please note: Mitchell Lake adjacent to the property is privately owned. We do not have permission to boat on the lake.

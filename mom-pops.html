Inquire About This Property

Escape to Peace & Nature.

Clean, spacious living on a peninsula surrounded by a beautiful, private man-made lake. Stunning views, incredible sounds of nature, Great Room, lofted ceilings, firepits, back deck, outdoor sectional.

Great WiFi, office space, TOY ROOM, Media room & living room. Washer/dryer, Central A/C, Keurig, 2 flatscreen TVs, Roku, Sonos Music, bikes, corn hole, air hockey.

Enjoy a break from the rest of the world. Halfway between NYC/Chicago.

The Space

Mom & Pop's is the perfect getaway for a 3-day weekend or longer stays. Enjoy the great room, gaze out the windows to see water surrounding the property on all three sides as it sits on a peninsula sprinkled with evergreens.

This private, clean, recently built lakefront home is beautifully decorated with a rustic, farmhouse feel and west-facing deck that will draw you each evening for sunset and later to the fire pit. A dock on site.

The finished basement is perfect for watching movies on the 65" flat screen while fully reclined in the leather sectional. Fun awaits in the playroom stocked with toys for all ages, an air hockey table, games, puzzles, and so much more. You'll never want to leave!

Perfect for multi-generational guests — teens and kids escape to the basement while adults find their happy place gazing out at the wildlife: bald eagles, muskrats, beavers, Canadian geese, 17 species of fish, snapping turtles, and lots of bullfrogs!

We do allow dogs! An additional $40 fee per dog.

Please note: We no longer have access to kayak or to use the lake for recreational use. We have many excellent options for swimming and kayaking nearby — please let us know and we can help you make arrangements.

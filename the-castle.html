Comfort Meets Style.

Newly remodeled 3 BR/2 bath home with lake views. Great WiFi. 2 decks, patio sectional furniture, fire pit, large yard — perfect for taking in great company and nature.

A stunning 6ft island/community table, designer convection oven, flat screen, 4-post bed. Bikes, fire pit. Craft breweries, golf, ski nearby.

BBQ grill, cozy, inviting, spacious — all you need for a perfect getaway.

The Space

The peace of waking to views of the water and hearing sounds of nature while enjoying the comforts of this stylish home will relax you. The back porch is perfect for a private dinner followed by a sunset toast overlooking the lake from the front deck.

We have a fire pit, BBQ, corn hole and bikes available.

We do allow dogs! An additional $40 fee per dog.

Please note: We no longer have access to kayak or to use the lake for recreational use. We have many excellent options for swimming and kayaking nearby.
